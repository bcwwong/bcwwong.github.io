    6.6 How to have one resource with different schedules

    • One resource with two different classes of users: ordinary and advanced users.
    • Ordinary users can only book the resource Mon to Fri 10:00 to 16:00.
    • Advanced users can additionally book the resource Mon to Fri 18:00 to 23:00 and Sat/Sun 09:00 to 23:00
