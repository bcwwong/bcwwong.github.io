    How to add new executive position and change the order of appearance of executive committees in Leadership page?

    https://www.sbms.hku.hk/leadership. New position to be created and order of appearance needed to be adjusted.

    Before editing:

    1. Log in as admin to CMS.

    2. Choose "Staff" under "Dynamic Modules":

    3. Search for staff (e.g., Enoch Chan) and go to "Staff Editing Page":

      1. Choose "Both" for "Show in*" to make staff appears in both Staff and Leadership pages
      2. Choose "Executive Committee" for "Leadership Category" (or choose "Scientific Advisory Board")

    4. Specify Executive Committee position. Choose an existing position in the "Position*" drop down menu or create new position, if necessary. To create a new position, type in the new position (e.g., Associate Director (Grants and Collaborations)).

    5. Publish the page: click "Save & Next", then "Preview", "Approve and Publish" and finally "OK" buttons.

    6. New Executive member added:

    7. Decide on the order of appearance (from left to right, top to bottom) of the executive committee memebrs:

      • Director - Michael Hausser
      • Associate Diector (Research and Innovation) - Martin Cheung
      • Associate Diector (Grants and Collaboration) - Enoch (e.g.)
      • Associate Diector (Knowledge Exchange & Global) - Stephanine Ma
      • Associate Diector (Teaching & Learning) - Cora Lai
      • Member (DY Jin)
      • Member (M Hu)

    8. Go back to Staff Listing Page. Click "Manage Display Sequence"

    9. On the "Staff Reorder list Page", click "Executive Committee"

    10. On the "Staff Reorder Page", click and drag the name of staff to arrange staff in the desired listing order.

    11. Publish the page: click "Save & Next", then "Preview", "Approve and Publish" and finally "OK" button.

    12. Finished page.
